# Enterprise GTM Framework

Practical frameworks for designing and operationalizing enterprise go-to-market strategies in complex B2B and enterprise environments.

## Purpose

This repository provides structured approaches for translating business growth priorities into actionable go-to-market decisions.

The frameworks are designed to support:

- market and regional prioritization,
- customer and segment strategy,
- Sales and Marketing alignment,
- scalable regional planning,
- performance measurement,
- organizational execution and adoption.

## My Point of View

In complex enterprise environments, GTM performance rarely fails because teams lack activities.

It usually fails because priorities, ownership, customer relevance and measurement are not sufficiently connected.

The frameworks in this repository are designed to close that gap by translating strategy into decisions, operating models and execution that teams can realistically adopt.

## Who This Is For

The materials are relevant for:

- B2B and enterprise marketing leaders,
- regional and field marketing teams,
- go-to-market and transformation leaders,
- Sales and Marketing stakeholders,
- organizations scaling across markets or customer segments.

## Core Principles

1. Start with business priorities, not marketing activities.
2. Prioritize markets and segments based on evidence.
3. Align Marketing and Sales around shared outcomes.
4. Balance global consistency with regional relevance.
5. Connect strategy with ownership, execution and measurement.
6. Build operating models that teams can realistically adopt.

## How to Use This Repository

1. Assess the current GTM situation.
2. Prioritize markets and segments.
3. Define differentiated segment strategies.
4. Align Marketing and Sales.
5. Build a regional execution plan.
6. Establish ownership and performance indicators.
7. Review results and adjust priorities.
