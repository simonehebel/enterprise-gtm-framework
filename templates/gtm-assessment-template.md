# GTM Assessment Template

## Purpose

This template supports a structured assessment of an organization's go-to-market situation.

It helps leadership teams identify strengths, gaps, risks and priorities across markets, customers, value propositions, commercial readiness and execution capabilities.

## How to Use This Template

Complete the sections with available evidence.

Mark assumptions clearly and identify where further validation is required.

The goal is not to produce a perfect document, but to create a transparent basis for GTM decisions.
