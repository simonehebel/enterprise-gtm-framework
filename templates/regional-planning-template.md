# Regional GTM Planning Template

## Purpose

This template supports the development of a structured regional go-to-market plan.

It helps regional Marketing, Sales and leadership teams translate global business priorities into locally relevant execution, ownership and measurable outcomes.

## How to Use This Template

Complete the sections using available market, customer, Sales and performance data.

Clearly distinguish between:

- confirmed facts,
- working assumptions,
- open questions,
- decisions still required.

The final plan should provide enough clarity for teams to execute, measure progress and adjust priorities.

A regional GTM plan should not be a list of disconnected marketing activities.

- regional business priorities,
- customer needs,
- account decisions,
- Sales and Marketing ownership,
- execution,
- performance measurement.
